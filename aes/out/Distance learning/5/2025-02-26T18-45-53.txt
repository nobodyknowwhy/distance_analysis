Distance learning is a way of studying where the teacher and students are not in the same place at the same time. Instead, classes can be taken online through special computers or phones connected to the internet.

I remember when I took an online class during the pandemic. We used platforms like Zoom or Moodle to attend lessons. However, distance learning also has some challenges. Sometimes it’s hard to stay focused, especially if you don’t have a quiet place to study at home. Also, without face-to-face interaction, it feels less personal compared to regular classes.

It also helps when you need extra help because the teacher can respond quickly through messages or video calls.
