Distance learning is a method of education where students attend classes or study from home instead of going to school in person. It has become more popular in recent years because of its flexibility and convenience.

Another benefit is that distance learning provides access to a wide range of resources. Students can choose from many online platforms and tools to learn new skills or explore different subjects. Despite these challenges, distance learning has grown a lot over the years and continues to become more advanced.
