One significant advantage of distance learning is its flexibility in time management. Additionally, it provides access to a wide array of resources from various institutions, expanding one's educational horizons beyond the confines of a traditional classroom setting.

Despite these challenges, distance learning has revolutionized education by integrating technology into the curriculum.
