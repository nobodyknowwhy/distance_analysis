One major benefit of distance learning is its ability to accommodate a wide range of learners. Another potential problem is technical issues, such as connectivity problems or software malfunctions, which can disrupt the learning process.

In conclusion, distance learning has transformed the way education is delivered, offering both opportunities and hurdles for students and educators alike.
