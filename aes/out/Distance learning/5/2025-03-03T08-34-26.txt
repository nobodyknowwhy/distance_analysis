Distance learning has become increasingly popular in recent years due to advancements in technology. It offers a flexible alternative for education, allowing students from various locations to engage in learning through digital platforms.

One significant challenge with distance learning is maintaining engagement without face-to-face interaction. Students might find it harder to stay motivated when they can't physically see or hear their instructors and peers. Additionally, technical issues such as unstable internet connectivity can disrupt the learning process for both instructors and students.

Despite these challenges, distance learning has numerous benefits. It provides flexibility, enabling students to study at their own pace and from any location with internet access.
