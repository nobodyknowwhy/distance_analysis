**Understanding Distance Learning**

Distance learning has become an essential part of modern education, especially with technology advancing rapidly. It allows students to learn from anywhere without physical classrooms, providing flexibility and convenience.

One method is synchronous learning, where lessons happen in real-time through video calls or webinars. This setup enables immediate interaction with teachers and peers, fostering engagement during sessions.

Another approach is asynchronous learning, which involves pre-recorded lectures or online forums for student discussions at their own pace. This method offers the benefit of reviewing content multiple times and participating in asynchronous interactions.
