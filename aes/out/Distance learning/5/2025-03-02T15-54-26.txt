In recent years, the advent of advanced technology has revolutionized education by introducing a new method: distance learning. This innovative approach allows students from diverse locations to engage in educational programs through internet-based platforms, breaking away from traditional classroom settings.

One significant advantage of distance learning is its flexibility. Students can access lessons at their convenience, making it ideal for those with busy schedules or physical constraints.
