Distance learning is an innovative approach to education that allows students to study without needing to be physically present in a traditional classroom. This method often occurs online, enabling students to access courses from their homes or other locations. Despite its benefits, distance learning has its challenges. Without face-to-face interaction with teachers and peers, students might feel isolated or lose motivation.
