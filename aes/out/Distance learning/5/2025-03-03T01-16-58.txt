Distance learning refers to the method of studying without attending a traditional classroom. It allows students from various locations to access educational content through digital platforms and online resources.

This approach also accommodates different learning styles, allowing visual learners to benefit from recorded lectures while auditory learners can listen to podcasts.

Despite these drawbacks, distance learning has become a valuable asset in education, especially during challenging times when schools were closed for health reasons.
