**Essay on Distance Learning**

Distance learning is a method of education where students learn without meeting face-to-face. It uses technology for teaching and learning online. This approach offers convenience, allowing students to study from home.

One key advantage is the cost-effectiveness. University courses can be accessed online at a lower cost compared to traditional classrooms. Additionally, it provides flexibility, enabling students to attend classes when they are most comfortable.

However, challenges exist too. Interaction with teachers and peers may be harder since learning isn't done in person. This might reduce focus as there's no immediate feedback or collaboration during sessions.

In summary, distance learning offers benefits like convenience and cost savings but faces difficulties in interaction and engagement, which could affect academic performance. As technology advances, more schools may adopt online methods to enhance education globally.
