Distance learning has become an increasingly popular educational method over the years. It allows individuals to attend classes from their homes without traditional school settings. This innovative approach offers flexibility and accessibility to education for many.

One reason it gained popularity is due to technological advancements, which made online platforms more widespread. The COVID-19 pandemic also accelerated its adoption as schools shifted to remote learning models.
