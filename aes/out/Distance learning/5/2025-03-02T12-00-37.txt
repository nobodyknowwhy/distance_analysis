Distance learning refers to an educational approach where students engage with lessons outside traditional classroom settings, often online or through various digital platforms. This method offers flexibility, allowing students to study from any location without worrying about commuting. For those with families or other commitments, this accessibility can be particularly beneficial.

However, distance learning also presents challenges. Without face-to-face interaction, it can be harder to stay focused and maintain motivation without a structured routine at home. Additionally, technical issues such as internet connectivity or device malfunction can disrupt the learning process for some students.
