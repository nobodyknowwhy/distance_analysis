**Essay on Distance Learning**

Distance learning has become increasingly popular as an educational method. It allows students to study remotely from home or other locations, offering a lot of flexibility in their schedules.

One major advantage is the accessibility to learning materials online. Students can access textbooks, videos, and other resources anytime they want, which is especially helpful for those juggling work or family commitments.
