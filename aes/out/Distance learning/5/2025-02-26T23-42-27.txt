Distance learning has emerged as an innovative approach to education, offering flexibility and accessibility that traditional classroom settings cannot provide. One significant advantage of distance learning is its flexibility. Additionally, it offers accessibility to regions with limited educational resources, ensuring that remote areas can still benefit from quality education.
