**Distance Learning: A Flexible Way to Learn**

Distance learning is a method of education where students learn without attending a physical classroom. It's become increasingly popular with the help of technology, like internet and online tools such as Zoom.

One advantage is its flexibility; students can attend classes from home or other locations, which is helpful if they have work or family commitments. Teachers use videos and interactive tools to make learning engaging remotely.

However, challenges exist too. Without seeing teachers face-to-face, it's harder to stay focused. Technical issues might also occur during lessons.
