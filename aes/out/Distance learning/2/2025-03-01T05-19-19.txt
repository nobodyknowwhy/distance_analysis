Distance learning refers to the method of conducting classes online, allowing students to attend school remotely without commuting to a physical location. This approach offers flexibility by enabling students to study at their own pace or from home during breaks.

One advantage is the convenience it provides. Students can access lessons anytime and from any place with internet connectivity, making education more accessible, especially for those with family responsibilities or jobs.

However, distance learning also presents challenges. Without face-to-face interaction, students might find it harder to stay motivated without external encouragement. Self-discipline becomes crucial as they need to manage their time effectively.
