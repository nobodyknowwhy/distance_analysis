**Distance Learning: A Flexible Approach**

Distance learning is a method of education where students learn from home without regular classes. It offers flexibility, which can help those with jobs or families manage their time better. Students can attend school at convenient times and save on travel costs and time.

However, distance learning also has challenges. Students might face distractions from home devices like TVs or computers. Additionally, there's less personal interaction between students and teachers compared to traditional classes.
