**Essay on Distance Learning**

Distance learning is a way of studying without being in a classroom. It’s good because you can study from anywhere you want, like at home or while traveling.

One advantage is that it offers flexibility with your study time and place. You can learn when suits you best, which helps manage your schedule better. Plus, if the lessons are recorded, you can watch them again later for better understanding.
