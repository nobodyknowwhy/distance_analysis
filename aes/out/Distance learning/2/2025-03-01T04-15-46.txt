**Essay on Distance Learning**

Distance learning is a way of studying online without attending school in person. It became popular during the pandemic but has grown as more people need flexible ways to learn.

Nowadays, distance learning uses computers and internet for classes and assignments. Platforms like Moodle help organize lessons, while tools such as Zoom assist with live sessions. This method offers flexibility, allowing students to study from home at any time.
