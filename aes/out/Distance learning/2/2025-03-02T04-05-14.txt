Distance learning refers to the educational method where students attend school or receive training through non-conventional methods, such as correspondence courses or computer-based systems, rather than traditional classroom instruction in a physical location. It also offers convenience for students who may not have access to quality educational opportunities in their local area. Additionally, modern technologies like video conferencing and online platforms make remote learning as effective as on-campus instruction.
