**Essay on Distance Learning**

Hello! My friend told me about something called distance learning... I'm not exactly sure what that is. From what I understand, it's when you learn online instead of going to school in person.

Despite these challenges, I think distance learning has its benefits. It allows you to learn from home at any time you want... without having to follow a strict schedule every day. This flexibility is really convenient for people who have busy lives or need more time to study.
