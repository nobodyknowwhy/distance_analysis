**Distance Learning: A New Way of Learning**

Distance learning is a method of education where students attend classes and study materials from a distance, instead of being physically present in a classroom. It has become more popular recently due to various reasons such as the pandemic, limited time for studies, or mobility issues.

One benefit of distance learning is that it gives students more flexibility. They can study at their own pace and schedule, which allows them to balance work, family, and personal life better. However, there are also challenges. For example, some students may find it harder to stay motivated without face-to-face interaction with teachers and classmates.
