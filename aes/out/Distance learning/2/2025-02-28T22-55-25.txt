**Distance Learning**

Distance learning refers to studying at home through the internet instead of attending school classes physically. It allows students to attend lessons from their own homes, using online tools like Zoom or Google Classroom.

One benefit is that students can learn without worrying about being in a classroom. However, there are challenges too. For example, students might get distracted by things like family members at home or lack the concentration without seeing their teacher face-to-face.

Despite these challenges, distance learning can still be effective if students stay organized and use tools properly. Teachers often adjust their methods to help students learn better online, maybe using emails or online platforms for extra support.

In summary, while it’s not always easy, distance learning is a useful way to continue studying when regular school isn’t possible.
