In today's world, distance learning has become increasingly popular. Unlike traditional classrooms, where students attend school in person, distance learning allows them to study from home or anywhere they have internet access.

For example, a student who works a part-time job can still attend class by watching videos and doing assignments online. This flexibility also means that students can learn at their own pace.
