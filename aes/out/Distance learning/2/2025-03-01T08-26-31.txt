**Distance Learning: A Flexible Way of Learning**

Distance learning refers to an educational method where students learn through materials provided online, often from home. It has become more popular recently, especially during the pandemic, but it was not always widely adopted before.

One advantage is that it offers flexibility, allowing students to study at their own pace and convenience. However, some challenges come with it, like staying motivated without regular interactions with instructors or peers.

Another benefit is increased accessibility, enabling people in remote areas to access courses that might otherwise be unavailable. Despite these advantages, distance learning may not suit everyone.
