**Distance Learning**

Distance learning is a way people learn without going to school in person. Instead of going to classes at a specific place and time, students can study from home or other places through online programs. It helps people learn new things even if they cannot go to school because of work, health, or family reasons.

When you take distance learning classes, you usually have access to the same materials as regular schools—books, videos, and online lessons. You can watch videos at your own time and do homework when it’s convenient for you. Some people like this kind of learning because they can study whenever they want without missing any important days at school.
