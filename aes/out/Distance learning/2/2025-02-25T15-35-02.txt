Distance learning is a method of education that occurs online, allowing students to study from home or other locations. This approach became widely popular during the COVID-19 pandemic when many schools and universities switched to online classes.

One advantage of distance learning is its flexibility. Students can attend classes whenever they want without strict schedules, making it easier for them to manage their time. Also, it enables students from different countries to learn together in the same classroom through video calls or online platforms.

Technology plays a significant role in this method because it allows people far apart to study together. For example, schools in various parts of the world now use distance learning to connect students globally.

However, not everyone has access to reliable technology at home. Some may lack internet connectivity or don't have the necessary devices like computers or laptops.
