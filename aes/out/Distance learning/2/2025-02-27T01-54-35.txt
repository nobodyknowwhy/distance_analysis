Distance learning is a method of studying without attending a physical school or university. It typically involves online classes and recorded lessons. The purpose is to provide education for those who can't attend traditional schools due to health issues or distance.

One advantage is its flexibility, allowing students to study anytime, balancing work and family life. Another benefit is accessibility to diverse educational opportunities from different countries through home schooling.

However, challenges include staying focused without a classroom, with distractions like TV or the internet. Technical issues can also hinder progress.

Another challenge is feeling isolated, as face-to-face interaction with peers is limited in online settings.

It's crucial to have support for both students and teachers to make distance learning effective. With appropriate help, it can be a valuable learning tool.
