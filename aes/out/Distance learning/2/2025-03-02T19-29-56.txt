**Distance Learning: A New Way of Learning**

Distance learning refers to the method of education where students and teachers meet outside of a traditional classroom setting. It allows people to learn from home or any other place with internet access. This type of learning is becoming more popular nowadays, especially during times when schools are closed because of health concerns.

They can study at their own pace and schedule, which means they can complete assignments and exams when it’s most convenient for them. This is great for people who have busy lives or need to take breaks from school.

Another advantage is that technology plays a big role in distance learning. Tools like video conferencing allow teachers to teach live online, just like face-to-face classes. Overall, distance learning is a new way of studying that offers flexibility and convenience. While there are challenges, like staying focused and feeling isolated at times, it can still be an effective way to continue learning even when schools aren’t in session.
