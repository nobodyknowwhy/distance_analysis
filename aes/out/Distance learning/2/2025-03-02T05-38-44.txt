One of the most significant advantages is that it offers great flexibility for students. They can study at their own pace, without being limited by time or location.

Another advantage of distance learning is its convenience. Students who have busy schedules or need to take care of family members can benefit from this type of education. For example, a student who works long hours after school may find it difficult to attend traditional classes on campus. For example, a student with mobility issues may find it easier to attend online classes than traveling long distances to attend in-person lessons.
