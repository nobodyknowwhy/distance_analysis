**Distance Learning: An Overview**

Distance learning refers to an educational method where lessons are delivered remotely, often over the internet. This approach allows students to study from home or any location with internet access. It offers flexibility by letting students learn at their own pace and revisit lectures.

One major benefit is convenience; students can balance work and studies more effectively. Courses are diverse, making it easier to explore various subjects from different universities worldwide.

Despite its advantages, challenges exist such as limited internet connectivity and technical issues. Some may struggle with motivation due to the absence of face-to-face interaction. Additionally, not everyone has reliable devices or internet access.
