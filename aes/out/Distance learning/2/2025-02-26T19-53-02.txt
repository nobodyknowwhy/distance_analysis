**Distance Learning**

Distance learning refers to a method of education where students attend classes and engage in academic activities from outside a traditional classroom setting. Instead of meeting in person with teachers and other students, they use technology, such as computers or the internet, to access course materials and communicate.

One benefit of distance learning is its flexibility. Students can attend classes at their own pace and schedule, which can be helpful for those who have other commitments, like work or family responsibilities. Another advantage is that it allows students from different regions to learn together without having to travel long distances.

However, distance learning also has some challenges. For example, students might find it harder to stay motivated since they are not physically present in a classroom with their peers and teachers.
