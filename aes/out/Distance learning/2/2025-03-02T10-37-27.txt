**Distance Learning: A New Way of Learning**

Distance learning is a method where students learn without meeting face-to-face. Instead, they access lessons online through the internet.

To study online, students use websites with virtual classrooms. These classrooms are like digital spaces where teachers and students can interact. Students watch videos, read articles, and participate in discussions all from home or another location.

One advantage of distance learning is its flexibility. Students can attend classes at any time they choose, which helps them manage their schedules better. It's also convenient for those who live far from schools. Plus, there are many resources available online that students can use to learn new skills or explore subjects in depth.

However, distance learning can be challenging. Sometimes the internet might not work well, and if you don't have a computer or internet access, it won't help you study.
