Distance learning refers to educational methods that occur through non-conventional means of communication. Unlike traditional classroom settings, distance learning allows students to engage in education from anywhere within their own environment.

One of the main challenges of distance learning is managing self-discipline and time management without a structured schedule. Students often face difficulties balancing their academic responsibilities with personal obligations, such as work or family duties. Additionally, some students feel they're behind their peers who attend physical classrooms due to the flexibility offered by online platforms.

Despite these challenges, many students find distance learning to be beneficial. It provides convenience and flexibility for those who may have other commitments outside of school hours. In conclusion, while distance learning presents its unique challenges, it has become an increasingly popular option for many students around the world due to advancements in technology and communication.
