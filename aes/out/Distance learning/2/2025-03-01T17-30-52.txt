Distance learning refers to an educational approach where students do not meet regularly at a physical location. Instead, they use technology like the internet or teleconferencing to connect and learn.

The concept of distance learning has become increasingly popular recently. It was first introduced in 1924 with “Distance Education,” established by Robert G. Innes. Over time, it has evolved into what we know today as an innovative method of education that offers flexibility for students.

One of the main advantages of distance learning is its accessibility. Students can attend classes from anywhere they have internet access.
