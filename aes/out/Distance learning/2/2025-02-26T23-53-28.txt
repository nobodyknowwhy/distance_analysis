This is especially helpful for people who have other commitments, like work or family responsibilities. Without these interactions, it can be harder for students to feel motivated or connected to their learning. I also worry that relying on online resources alone might not provide a well-rounded education.

In conclusion, distance learning has its benefits, like flexibility and accessibility. But I think it should not replace traditional classroom learning entirely. It’s important to find a balance between the two so that students can get the most out of their education.
