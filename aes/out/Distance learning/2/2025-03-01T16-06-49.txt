Distance learning is a method of education where students do not attend school regularly but instead receive instruction through various non-traditional methods. It has become increasingly popular in recent years, especially as technology advanced.

The origins of distance learning can be traced back to the mid-20th century when early educational technologies began to emerge. Today, it includes a wide range of formats such as online courses, correspondence courses, and e-learning programs.
