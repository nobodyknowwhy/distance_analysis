**Distance Learning: A Way to Learn While Lifestyles Change**

Distance learning is an online method where students study from home or other places without meeting in one place. It's like having classes from your own room on a computer.

This approach offers flexibility, allowing people to choose their own time for lessons, which helps if they have jobs or family matters. Plus, it can be used because of being far from schools due to moving or health issues.
