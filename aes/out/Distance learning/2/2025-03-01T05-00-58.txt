Distance learning is a way of education where students learn from home instead of going to school every day. It is usually done through the internet or other digital devices. This type of learning has become more popular in recent years because it offers many benefits for students and schools.

One benefit of distance learning is that it makes education accessible to people who live far away from a school. For example, a student in another country can attend a university without needing to travel long distances. It also gives students the flexibility to study at their own pace or when they feel most comfortable.
