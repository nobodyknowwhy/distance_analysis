Distance learning has become increasingly popular in recent years as schools and colleges explore new ways to deliver education to students who may not be able to attend classes in person. One of the main advantages of distance learning is its flexibility. It allows students to attend classes from home, which can be particularly beneficial for those with busy schedules or families.
