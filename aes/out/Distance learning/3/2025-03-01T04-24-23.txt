**Essay on Distance Learning**

Distance learning has become increasingly popular in recent years, offering a new way of studying from home. Instead of going to school every day, students can access classes online through computer systems and video calls.

One advantage is the convenience it offers. Many people have busy lives with jobs or families, making traditional schooling difficult. Distance learning allows them to study at their own pace without worrying about class times.
