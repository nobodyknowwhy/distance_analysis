**Distance Learning**

Distance learning is a way of teaching and learning that happens over the internet or other digital tools without students meeting in person. It allows students to study from home or another place while teachers teach them online. This type of learning has become more popular because of things like the pandemic, which forced schools to close down.

One advantage of distance learning is that it gives students a lot of flexibility. They can learn at their own pace and take breaks when they need them. This helps some students who have other things going on in their lives or are not able to go to school regularly.

However, distance learning can also be hard for teachers because they have to teach students who are at home without having the same kind of interaction as a classroom. Some teachers say it’s challenging to keep up with how students are doing and help them when they need it.

Overall, distance learning is a good way for people to learn even if they can’t go to school in person. It helps everyone be more flexible and continue their education from home.
