Distance learning has emerged as an innovative approach to education, allowing students and teachers to connect asynchronously through digital platforms. Today, with advancements in technology and internet connectivity, distance learning has transformed into a robust method of delivering educational content to a broader audience.

One of the primary advantages of distance learning is its flexibility. Despite its many benefits, distance learning is not without its challenges.
