Distance learning is a form of education that allows students to attend classes remotely through technology like the internet or telephones. It has become increasingly popular in recent years due to its flexibility and accessibility. However, it also comes with challenges.

One challenge is isolation. Students may feel lonely because they are not physically present in a classroom. Another challenge is technical issues if their devices do not work properly. Additionally, students need good self-discipline to stay focused on their studies without supervision.

Despite these challenges, distance learning has its benefits. It provides students with more flexibility, allowing them to study at their own pace and from anywhere they have internet access. Distance learning has been around for a long time. It started in the early 20th century when universities began offering online courses through teletext. Today, many schools and colleges use video conferencing tools like Zoom or Moodle to deliver lessons remotely.
