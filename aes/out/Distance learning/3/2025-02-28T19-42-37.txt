Distance learning is an educational method where students learn without stepping foot in a classroom. It allows education from places that aren't reachable by traditional schools. This form of learning has become increasingly popular due to technological advancements.

Despite these challenges, distance learning is seen as a positive alternative for those who prefer not to attend school regularly. It continues to evolve with technological improvements aiming to enhance its effectiveness.
