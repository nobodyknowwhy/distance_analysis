**Distance Learning: A Flexible Educational Tool**

Distance learning is an educational approach where students learn without meeting face-to-face. It's often conducted online, allowing flexibility and convenience.

One advantage of distance learning is its ability to help people with disabilities continue their education. Additionally, it has become increasingly popular as remote work became more common during the COVID-19 pandemic. This mode of learning also offers convenience for those who prefer to study at home or in various locations.

However, there are challenges associated with distance learning. Technical issues, such as poor internet connectivity, can hinder progress. Moreover, distractions at home and reduced face-to-face interaction may affect learning outcomes compared to traditional classroom settings.
