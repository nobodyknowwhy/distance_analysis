Distance learning has become an increasingly popular method of education in recent years. It refers to a type of educational instruction conducted through non-interactive computer-based systems or other electronic means. This form of learning allows students to study from home or any location without needing to attend a traditional classroom.

The ability to learn from home also gives students more time to study and complete assignments. However, distance learning may not be suitable for everyone. Additionally, technical issues can sometimes arise, such as connectivity problems or software malfunctions, which might disrupt the learning process.
