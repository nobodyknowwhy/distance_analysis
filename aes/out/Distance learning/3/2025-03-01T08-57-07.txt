**Distance Learning: An Overview**

Distance learning is a method of studying without attending classes in person. Traditionally, education was done face-to-face, but now many have moved online due to technology.

One advantage is the flexibility students have. They can attend classes from home or any place with internet access. This allows for personalizing their schedule and accessing materials whenever they want.

Another benefit is learning at one's own pace. Students can review topics they find challenging without feeling rushed, and they can revisit lessons anytime to refresh their memory.

However, distance learning also has challenges. Students may face distractions like noise or problems with internet connectivity while studying from home. These issues can make it harder to focus and complete tasks effectively.

In conclusion, distance learning has transformed education by making learning more flexible but also presents its own set of difficulties that students need to manage.
