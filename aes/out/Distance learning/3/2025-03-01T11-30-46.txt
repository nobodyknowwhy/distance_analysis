**Essay on Distance Learning**

Distance learning is an educational method that allows people to study from their homes, often through the internet or phone. It’s like having a teacher right at your door without leaving your comfort zone.

One big benefit of distance learning is its flexibility. You can learn whenever you want, which is great if you have family responsibilities or work commitments. Plus, it helps people in remote areas get an education even if there are no schools nearby.
