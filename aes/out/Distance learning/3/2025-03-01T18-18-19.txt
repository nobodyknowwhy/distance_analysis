**Distance Learning: An Overview**

Distance learning refers to a method of education where students learn from home instead of attending classes in person. It’s becoming more popular today, especially with the help of technology.

Nowadays, many people use distance learning for various reasons. For example, some students can’t attend school because they’re sick or far away. During the pandemic, it became even more common as schools and universities moved online.

One advantage is that you can study at your own pace. This might make learning easier since you don't have to wait for others in a class. Also, using technology makes it convenient; many online classes are available through websites or apps.

Also, sometimes there aren’t enough internet connections where people live, which can cause problems with taking classes. Another thing is that you don’t get the same face-to-face interaction as in a regular classroom, which might make learning harder.
