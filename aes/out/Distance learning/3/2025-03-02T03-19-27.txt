**Understanding Distance Learning**

Distance learning refers to an educational method where students attend classes remotely, often online. This approach offers flexibility, allowing individuals to study from home while adhering to their schedules.

One of its key advantages is the ability to attend classes without physical presence in a traditional classroom. Additionally, students can access diverse perspectives by participating with learners from different regions around the world.

However, distance learning also presents challenges, such as reliance on reliable internet connectivity and necessary technology. Without proper devices or stable networks, technical issues may arise, affecting the learning experience.

Furthermore, while the convenience of online classes is undeniable, it can lead to feelings of isolation among students who are not physically present in a classroom setting, potentially impacting engagement levels during lessons.
