Distance learning has become increasingly popular in recent years, especially due to technological advancements. It allows students from anywhere in the world to learn without needing a physical classroom. This method is becoming more accessible and diverse as technology continues to grow.

Additionally, there are an abundance of resources available online, providing students with access to a wide variety of materials at no cost.
