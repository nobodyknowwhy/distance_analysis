**Understanding Distance Learning**

Distance learning is an educational method where students and teachers communicate online instead of meeting face-to-face. This means learning can happen from home or anywhere with internet access. It often uses video calls or online platforms for classes.

One way it works is through video conferencing, like Zoom, to attend lessons remotely. Platforms such as Moodle are used for course materials and assignments.

Distance learning has many benefits. It offers flexibility, allowing students to study at their own pace. It also lets people access courses from experts worldwide, expanding opportunities. However, challenges include technical issues with internet or devices, and distractions like noise at home.
