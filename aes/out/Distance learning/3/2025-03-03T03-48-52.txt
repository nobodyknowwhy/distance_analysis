**Understanding Distance Learning**

Distance learning is an educational method where students learn without attending classes on-site. Instead, they study from home through computers or the internet.

People choose this approach because of their work or family responsibilities, allowing flexibility in their schedules.

While online learning can be isolating and harder to interact with others, it offers convenience as you can study anytime, saving time and money.

Despite challenges like fewer interactions with teachers and classmates, distance learning is popular for its ease and focus-enhancing benefits. Overall, while there are some drawbacks, it’s helpful for many individuals looking for flexibility in their education.
