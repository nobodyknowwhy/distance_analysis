**Distance Learning**

Distance learning refers to a method of education where students and teachers interact remotely, often through technology like computers or the internet. It allows people to learn from home or in other locations while maintaining their usual routines.

One major challenge with distance learning is ensuring that all students have consistent access to reliable technology, such as internet connectivity. If a student loses their internet connection during class, they may miss important lessons and fall behind. Additionally, some students might find it difficult to stay motivated without the structure of regular school hours.

Another advantage of distance learning is its flexibility. However, this independence can also lead to challenges in maintaining a routine and staying focused on assignments.

Overall, while distance learning has its pros and cons, it provides a lot of freedom and convenience for students who may not have the option to attend traditional schools.
