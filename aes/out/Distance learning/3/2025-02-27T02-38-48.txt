**Distance Learning: An Introduction**

Distance learning is a method of studying that happens online. Instead of going to school, students attend classes from home using computers or mobile phones. They can access lessons anytime and anywhere. This approach has become popular because many people need to study while working, especially if they're away from their schools.

One advantage is the flexibility it offers. Students can learn at their own pace without worrying about other subjects. It also saves time on commuting for classes. Some choose this method because they have families or jobs that require long hours, offering more convenience.

However, there are challenges too. Without face-to-face interaction with teachers and classmates, some students feel less connected. Additionally, technical issues can arise when trying to join online classes, which might disrupt the learning process.

In summary, distance learning is a useful option for many, but it does require adjustments to handle potential social isolation and technical problems effectively.
