Distance learning is a type of education that students can receive through studying from home or other remote locations, rather than attending a traditional classroom. It has become more popular in recent years because it offers flexibility and convenience for students.

One advantage of distance learning is that students can study at their own pace without being limited by time or location. This is especially helpful for people who have busy schedules or need to balance work and family life with their studies. Additionally, distance learning allows students to access a wider range of courses from different universities around the world.
