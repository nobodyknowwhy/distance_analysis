Distance learning refers to a method of education where students and teachers are separated geographically but maintain contact through written or electronic communication. It has become increasingly popular in recent years due to its flexibility and accessibility.

Additionally, distance learning allows people in remote areas access to education that may not be available locally. Despite its advantages, however, distance learning can sometimes present challenges such as lack of face-to-face interaction and potential distractions.
