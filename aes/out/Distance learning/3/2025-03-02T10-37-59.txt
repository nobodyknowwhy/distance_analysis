Distance learning refers to a method of education where students attend classes or receive instruction through non-traditional means, such as television or the internet. Unlike traditional classroom settings, distance learning allows students to learn from home or other locations.

One benefit of distance learning is its flexibility. Students can attend classes at their own pace and schedule, which can be helpful for balancing school with work or family responsibilities.
