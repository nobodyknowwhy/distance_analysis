**Distance Learning**

Distance learning refers to education that takes place over a distance or online. It allows people to study without meeting in person. Instead of attending classes at a school, students can access lessons from home through the internet.

One advantage of distance learning is that it gives people more flexibility. They can learn at their own pace and on their own time. This can be helpful for people who have busy schedules or need to balance work and family life with their studies.

However, distance learning also has some challenges. For example, students may not get the same level of interaction as they would in a traditional classroom.
