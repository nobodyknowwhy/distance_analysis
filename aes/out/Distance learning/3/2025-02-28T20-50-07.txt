**Distance Learning: A New Way of Learning**

Distance learning is when you learn online from home. It's helpful for people who can’t attend school because of health issues or family.

Some people have health problems that make it hard to go to school in person, so they study at home instead. Distance learning allows them to continue their education without missing out on important classes.

Because of the COVID-19 pandemic, many people are using online learning more than ever before. It’s also useful for those who have jobs or families that take them away from home during the day, making it easier for them to study at home.

It's important for everyone to stay connected and learn through different ways like online learning now more than ever.
