**Distance Learning**

Distance learning is a way of studying without going to a school classroom. Instead, students can study from home or other places through the internet or phone. This type of learning has become more popular because it gives people more freedom to study.

One advantage of distance learning is that it allows students to learn at their own pace. They can watch videos and listen to lessons at any time they want. Some people also like this because it lets them spend more time with family or hobbies. Another benefit is that it helps students focus better on their studies because there are no distractions from other classmates.

However, distance learning also has some challenges. Sometimes the internet might not work well, and students have to be careful while doing their homework.
