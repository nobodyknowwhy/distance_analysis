Distance learning is a way of teaching where students learn without meeting face-to-face with their teachers or classmates in a physical classroom. Instead, they use technology and the internet to connect online. This method allows people from different places to study together.

One advantage of distance learning is its flexibility. Students can choose when and where to study, which is great for those who have jobs or families that make it hard to attend school regularly. It also gives access to education that might be difficult to get otherwise.

However, there are some challenges too. Sometimes students may find it hard to stay focused without classmates around them, and they can't talk face-to-face with their teachers as much. This can make learning seem less interactive and more difficult at times.

Another issue is the risk of getting distracted by things like family or home work while studying. Also, technical problems sometimes happen which can be frustrating for students trying to learn. Despite these challenges, distance learning still plays an important role in education today because technology has made it more common.
