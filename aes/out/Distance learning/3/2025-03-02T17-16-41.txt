Distance learning is an innovative method of studying conducted remotely through digital devices instead of traditional classrooms. It has gained popularity due to technological advancements. While this approach offers flexibility in both time and space, it also presents certain challenges.

One significant advantage of distance learning is its convenience. Students can attend classes from home or any location without worrying about commuting. This eliminates the need for fixed school schedules, allowing students more flexibility to manage their time effectively.

However, distance learning is not without its drawbacks. One potential issue is the presence of distractions at home, such as television or mobile phones, which can affect focus during lessons. Additionally, technical problems like internet connectivity issues may disrupt the learning process.
