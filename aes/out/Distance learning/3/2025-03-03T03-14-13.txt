Distance learning is a method of education where students interact remotely from home or other locations. It has become increasingly popular due to technological advancements and unforeseen circumstances like the COVID-19 pandemic.

One major challenge in distance learning is limited access to resources, such as libraries and tech support, which can hinder progress for some students. Additionally, technical issues like internet problems may disrupt learning. Another issue is isolation, as students may feel disconnected from peers without face-to-face interactions.

Despite these challenges, distance learning offers several benefits. It provides flexibility, allowing students to learn at their own pace without strict schedules.
