**Distance Learning: A New Way of Learning**

Distance learning is a way of learning that happens when you are not in a traditional classroom. Instead, you can study at home or anywhere else through the internet. It’s like having your own teacher who comes to you!  

Why is this type of learning becoming more popular? Well, maybe it’s because people want to learn new things without missing work or school. Or perhaps they have health problems that make going to class hard. Distance learning can help them study from home.
