Distance learning is a way to learn from home without going to school. It's conducted online or through materials sent by mail. This method allows students to attend classes from their own homes because everything they need, like lessons and assignments, is provided either through the internet or mail.

One advantage of distance learning is its flexibility. Students can study at any time that suits them best, which helps with work or family commitments. It's also beneficial for people in remote areas who might not have access to traditional schools.
