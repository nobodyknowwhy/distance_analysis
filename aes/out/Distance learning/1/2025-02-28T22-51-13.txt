**Distance learning** is a way of studying without going to a school or university in person. Instead, you can attend classes online through the internet. This helps people who live far away from schools or can't come to school because of health problems.

With distance learning, students can study at their own pace and choose when they want to learn. This is good for everyone, especially those with jobs or family responsibilities that make it hard to go to school every day.

However, some challenges come with this type of learning. For example, you can’t always see your teachers or classmates the way you do in a regular classroom. Sometimes this makes it harder to pay attention and feel motivated. But overall, distance learning is becoming more popular because it gives people more flexibility and access to education they might not have had before.
