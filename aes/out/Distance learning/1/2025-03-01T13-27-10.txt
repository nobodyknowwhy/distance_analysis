Distance learning is a method of education where students learn without physically attending classrooms. Instead, they access classes remotely through technology. This approach offers flexibility, allowing students to attend classes at any time.

One advantage of distance learning is its accessibility. Students can use their devices and internet connections to access materials anytime. Additionally, collaboration tools like forums or video calls help them interact with others in real-time.
