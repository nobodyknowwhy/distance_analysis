Distance learning refers to education conducted remotely, often through the internet, without a physical classroom. It allows students to study from home by accessing online classes.

Firstly, distance learning provides flexibility, enabling students to attend classes at convenient times. This is especially helpful for those with busy schedules or health issues. Moreover, it offers access to various subjects that may not be available locally, expanding educational opportunities.

However, some challenges exist. Interaction with teachers and peers can be limited compared to traditional classrooms. Additionally, distractions like noisy homes or technical issues might affect learning focus.

Despite these hurdles, distance learning is growing in popularity as more students find it beneficial for personal reasons. Its adaptability makes education accessible to a wider audience, though challenges remain.
