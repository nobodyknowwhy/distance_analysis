**Distance Learning: An Introduction**

Distance learning is when you study without going to school. Instead of being in a classroom with other students, you learn at home or somewhere else on your own. It often uses the internet to get lessons online. You can watch videos and read books about your subject. Sometimes teachers send emails with assignments.

One good thing about distance learning is that it’s flexible. You can study whenever you want, even if school isn’t in session. But there are also some challenges. Without meeting others face-to-face, it might be harder to stay focused or ask questions when you don’t understand something. Also, it requires self-discipline because you have to manage your time well.

Distance learning has changed how we learn now. It’s a new way for people to get educated. However, it isn’t always easy and can cause some confusion. Still, it is helpful in many ways, even if there are some problems along the way.
