One benefit is that it offers flexibility in learning schedules, allowing students to attend classes at their own pace without strict timings. This means they can save time and money by not commuting regularly and have weekends free for other activities.

Distance learning also helps students focus better since there are no classmates around during lessons. However, challenges like bad internet connections or issues with equipment can arise. Exams might be taken online too, which could make privacy a concern. Motivation is crucial to keep studying consistently without distractions.
