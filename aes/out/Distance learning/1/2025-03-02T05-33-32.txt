Distance learning is a new way of learning without going to a physical classroom. Instead, students can attend classes online through the internet. This means they can watch lessons on videos and complete assignments using shared devices.

One challenge of distance learning is that it's harder to interact with teachers and classmates because everyone is in different places. Students might feel lonely since there's no face-to-face contact. Also, if someone doesn't have a good internet connection or the right tools at home, they might run into problems.

However, distance learning has some advantages. It gives students more flexibility; they can study from anywhere with internet access. This means they can learn at their own pace and maybe even take classes when they're not in school. Plus, teachers can easily track how well each student is doing since everything is done online.

In conclusion, while distance learning isn't perfect and has some challenges, its benefits make it an important part of modern education that many students find helpful now.
