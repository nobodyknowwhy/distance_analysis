It’s often used when you can’t go to school because of health problems, family issues, or other reasons. With the help of the internet and some special tools, people can learn things like reading, writing, math, and even college courses.

One advantage of distance learning is that it gives students more time for their hobbies or family responsibilities. But sometimes it’s hard to stay focused because there are no teachers or classmates around to help when you’re stuck on a problem. Also, if the internet doesn’t work well in your area, you might not be able to watch videos or take online tests.
