Distance learning is a type of education where students do not meet face-to-face with teachers or other students. It can be done online through computer systems or via other electronic means.  

One advantage of distance learning is that it provides flexibility in time. Students can study at their own pace and on their own schedule, which can be helpful if they have jobs or family obligations. Another advantage is the convenience of having access to learning materials from anywhere with an internet connection.

However, distance learning also has challenges. Students need to stay disciplined without a teacher or classmates around to keep them motivated. Additionally, technical issues like internet problems can sometimes arise, which can be frustrating for students who are trying to learn.
