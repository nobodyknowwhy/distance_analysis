**Understanding Distance Learning**

Distance learning refers to studying from home or another location instead of a traditional classroom. During COVID-19, many people found themselves using this method, making it popular for flexible education.

One key benefit is flexibility, allowing students to learn anywhere they have internet access. This is especially helpful for those unable to attend school regularly.
