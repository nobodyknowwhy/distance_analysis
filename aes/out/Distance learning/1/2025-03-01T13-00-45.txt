Distance learning is a way people learn without being physically present at a traditional school. Despite these challenges, distance learning has proven to be a valuable option for many people. It allows students to learn at their own pace and helps them manage their time better. Additionally, it opens up opportunities for people who live in remote areas or have other commitments that make traditional school attendance difficult.
