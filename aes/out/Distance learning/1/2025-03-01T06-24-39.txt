Distance learning has become a necessity due to recent health circumstances. It is a method of teaching where students attend school online or in specific locations away from their regular environment. This form of education can be conducted through various means such as video calls, online classes, and interactive platforms.

One of the main advantages of distance learning is its flexibility. Students who cannot attend school regularly due to health issues can continue their education without missing any lessons. Teachers use different tools to ensure that students stay engaged during the class, like chat boxes or shared screens for live interaction.

However, distance learning also has challenges. Without face-to-face communication, it can be harder for teachers and students to understand each other fully. There might also be technical issues with internet connectivity or access to necessary materials. Additionally, some students may find it difficult to stay motivated without the usual school environment.

Despite these challenges, many schools are adapting to this new format by using various technologies and strategies to make learning as effective as possible. Overall, distance learning has proven to be a reliable way for students to continue their education even when traditional schooling is not possible.
