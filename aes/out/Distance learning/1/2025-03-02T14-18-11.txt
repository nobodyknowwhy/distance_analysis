Distance learning is a way people learn things over the internet from their homes. It's different from regular school because you don't go to classes in person.

One good thing about distance learning is that it gives more time for families or jobs. Some people can study when they have free time, which helps them keep up with work or family. Also, it allows students to learn at their own pace, so they can take time to understand topics well.

But there are some problems too. Sometimes the internet doesn't work well, and you might not get as much help from teachers if you don't know something. It's also hard to talk with classmates when you're learning alone.

Even though it has its challenges, distance learning is helpful because it lets everyone stay connected and have access to education in a different way.
