Distance learning is a method where students study at home or another location instead of attending school regularly. It offers convenience by allowing people who can't attend in-person classes due to health issues or other reasons to learn from home.

One advantage is access to education through technology, such as the internet and devices like computers or tablets. This enables learning from anywhere, making it accessible to many students.

However, there are challenges, including distractions at home, difficulty staying focused without peers, and technical issues with devices. Additionally, students might miss out on face-to-face interaction with teachers and classmates.

Despite these challenges, technology plays a significant role in facilitating distance learning through tools like video conferencing software (e.g., Zoom or Skype) and online resources that enhance the learning experience.
