Some people choose distance learning because it’s very flexible. You can learn on your own schedule and take as much time as you need to understand the material. It also allows you to study in places that are convenient for you, like libraries or cafes.

However, there are some challenges with distance learning. For example, without face-to-face interaction with teachers and classmates, it might be harder to stay motivated.
