Distance learning is a way people can study at home through the internet. It allows students to attend classes from their own homes without going to a school. It also gives parents more time with their children, which is great for families.

Another advantage is that distance learning offers access to various subjects and courses that might not be available in local schools. Students can choose what they want to learn at their own pace, making it easier to balance school with other responsibilities like work or family.
