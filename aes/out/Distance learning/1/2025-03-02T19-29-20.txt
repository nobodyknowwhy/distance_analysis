Distance learning is an educational method where learning occurs online instead of in a traditional classroom setting. It has become quite popular recently, especially for those who can't attend school due to health issues or living far away.

One major advantage of distance learning is its flexibility. Students can study at any time and place they choose, which makes it ideal for balancing work and family responsibilities. This self-paced learning allows students to grasp concepts thoroughly without pressure from classmates.

Another benefit is accessibility. With distance learning, people worldwide can access quality education regardless of their location. This promotes inclusivity in education, giving everyone the chance to learn even if they don't have access to schools locally.
