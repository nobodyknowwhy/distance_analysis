Distance learning is a method of education where students learn at home through written materials and correspondence between teachers and students.

It allows people to study from anywhere, even if they are not physically present in a classroom. This is helpful because it gives students more flexibility in their schedules. For example, someone who works can continue working while studying.

However, distance learning also has some challenges. It can be harder for students to focus without the distractions of being in a physical classroom. Some people might find it difficult to stay motivated when they are not surrounded by peers and teachers face-to-face.

Some platforms like Moodle or Zoom can help with communication between teachers and students. But sometimes, technical issues can cause problems, such as slow internet connections or broken devices.
