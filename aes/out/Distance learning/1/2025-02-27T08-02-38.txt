Distance learning is a way of studying without going to school or university. Instead, students can study from home or another place online. It often happens because of bad health, moving houses, or something else that makes it hard to go to school.

Some people like distance learning because they don’t like to sit in a classroom every day. They can learn at their own pace and take breaks when they need them. But some students find it hard to stay focused without the help of teachers and classmates. They also have trouble with assignments or exams if they don’t know how to do them online.
