Distance learning is a method of education that allows individuals to study from home without attending traditional classrooms. It offers flexibility, making it easier for people to manage their schedules around work or family responsibilities.

One advantage is the ability to attend classes at any time, which can be beneficial for students seeking higher education. However, challenges like bad internet connectivity and distractions can occur, affecting learning outcomes.

Distance learning also differs from online schooling in that it often requires self-discipline to stay focused on assignments without constant supervision. It emphasizes independent study skills, such as managing time effectively.
