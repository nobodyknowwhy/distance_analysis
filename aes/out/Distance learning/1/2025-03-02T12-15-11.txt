Distance learning is a method of education where students interact with their teachers and peers outside a traditional classroom, typically using technology like computers and the internet. During the COVID-19 pandemic, many schools transitioned to online learning, highlighting its growing importance.

One significant benefit of distance learning is flexibility. Students can attend classes at any time that suits them best, which is especially helpful for those with jobs or family responsibilities.
