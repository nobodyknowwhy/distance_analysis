**Distance Learning**

Distance learning has become a significant method for education over the past few years. It allows students to attend classes from different places without needing to physically be in a traditional classroom. This type of learning is especially useful when people cannot go to school regularly, such as those with health issues or busy schedules.

One advantage of distance learning is its flexibility. Students can watch lessons at any time they want and replay them if they miss something important. This flexibility gives students more time to complete their tasks and study without worrying about missing deadlines due to personal reasons. However, the lack of face-to-face interaction between teachers and students can sometimes be a challenge. Without the opportunity for immediate questions or feedback from professors, it might take longer for students to fully grasp the material.
