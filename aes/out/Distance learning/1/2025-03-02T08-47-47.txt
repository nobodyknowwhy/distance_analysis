**Distance Learning: An Overview**

Distance learning is an online education method where students learn without being physically present on a university campus. It's ideal for individuals in remote areas or those with health issues preventing attendance.

One key benefit is flexibility, allowing students to balance work, family responsibilities, and personal time effectively. They can focus more since they're not distracted by classmates or teachers during class times.

However, challenges exist too. Technical issues like bad internet can disrupt learning, and some students find it harder to engage with others due to lack of face-to-face interaction. Assessments are often online, requiring preparation for various platforms.

In conclusion, distance learning offers flexibility but also has its difficulties related to technology and social interactions.
