**Distance Learning**

Distance learning is a way for students to study at home instead of going to school regularly. Instead of meeting with teachers and classmates every day, they attend classes online or watch videos.

I believe this helps because students can spend more time on homework and personal life. Some students might have health issues and can’t go to school when they’re sick.
