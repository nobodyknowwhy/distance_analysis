It refers to the process of teaching or learning that occurs remotely, often through technology such as the internet or telecommunication systems. This type of learning has gained popularity due to various reasons, including flexibility for students, convenience, and access to education from different parts of the world.

One of the main benefits of distance learning is its flexibility. Students can attend classes and complete assignments at their own pace without worrying about commuting to a physical location. This allows them to balance their studies with other commitments, such as work or family responsibilities. Additionally, distance learning often provides students with more personalized attention from teachers, as they can focus on individual progress rather than group instruction.

Another advantage is the diversity it offers. Students from different countries and regions can access high-quality education that might not be available in their local area. This broadens their perspective and enhances global understanding. However, distance learning also presents some challenges. Despite these challenges, the future of distance learning looks promising. Furthermore, personalized learning plans and adaptive learning systems are becoming more common, ensuring that each student receives tailored support based on their needs.
