Distance learning refers to a method of education where students do not meet regularly at a physical location with an instructor. Instead, they access course materials online or through other digital platforms. This type of learning has become increasingly popular in recent years due to its flexibility and accessibility.

One advantage of distance learning is that it allows students to attend school from home without needing to worry about commuting or finding a suitable classroom. Additionally, distance learning often provides a variety of courses at different levels, allowing people of all ages and backgrounds to access education.
