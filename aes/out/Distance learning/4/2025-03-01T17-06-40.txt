Distance learning has become a modern approach to education where classes are conducted remotely. Additionally, technology plays a crucial role in this approach, as platforms like Zoom and Moodle facilitate virtual interactions, making education accessible from anywhere with internet connectivity.

Managing time effectively becomes essential due to the self-discipline required to complete assignments without external supervision.
