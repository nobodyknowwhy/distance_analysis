Distance learning is an educational method where students access lessons remotely through technology, allowing them to study from home. Its purpose is to offer flexibility, enabling students with busy schedules or health issues to attend school without leaving their commitments.

One of the primary benefits of distance learning is its convenience. Students can attend school sessions at any time, fitting their studies around work or family obligations. This is particularly helpful for those juggling multiple responsibilities. Additionally, some students find it easier to learn subjects like science at home due to hands-on experiments and better focus in a controlled environment.

Despite these advantages, distance learning also presents challenges such as distractions from daily activities and technical issues that may arise if the student lacks tech proficiency. However, these can be mitigated with a clean study environment and using virtual backgrounds to maintain engagement.
