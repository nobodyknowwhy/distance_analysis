Distance learning refers to the method of education where students and teachers interact remotely through technology. It has become increasingly popular in recent years due to various reasons such as geographical limitations or the need for flexibility.

One of the main challenges of distance learning is maintaining effective communication between teachers and students. Despite these challenges, many institutions are making efforts to improve the quality of distance learning through better planning and resources.
