Distance learning is a way of studying that happens when people learn from their homes instead of going to a school. It can be done using the internet or other technology. Some people think it’s not as good as regular schools, but I believe it has many advantages.

Another thing I love about distance learning is that it gives people who live far apart the chance to learn together.
