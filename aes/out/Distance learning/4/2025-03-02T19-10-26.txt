Technical issues, such as internet outages or device malfunctions, can also disrupt learning experiences, adding stress to an already demanding situation.

**Benefits Beyond Access - Flexibility:**  
Despite its challenges, distance learning offers significant advantages, including the ability to study independently without adhering to rigid schedules. This flexibility caters to individuals juggling work commitments or family responsibilities, allowing them to progress at their own pace and manage their time effectively. Moreover, it opens doors to lifelong learning opportunities not typically available in traditional education settings.

**Conclusion:**  
In conclusion, distance learning has revolutionized access to education by expanding reach and accommodating diverse lifestyles.
