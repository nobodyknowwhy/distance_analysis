**Distance Learning: An Exploration of Its Evolution and Impact**

**Introduction:**  
Distance learning refers to a method of education where students and teachers are not in the same physical location during the learning process. It has evolved over time, adapting to technological advancements.

**Reasons for Popularity:**  
The rise in distance learning is largely due to technological progress, which has made it more accessible. It offers flexibility, allowing students to attend classes from home or other locations without fixed schedules.

**Example During Pandemic:**  
During the COVID-19 pandemic, many schools shifted to online learning, highlighting its growing importance and accessibility as a tool for education during crises.
