Distance learning refers to educational programs where students engage with content remotely, typically through technology such as the internet or telemedicine. Early examples include correspondence schools in Europe during the 19th century, which allowed people from different locations to study together without physical constraints. Despite these benefits, distance learning also presents challenges.
