Distance learning refers to educational methods conducted over the internet or other digital platforms without a traditional classroom setting. Its rise has been notable, especially post-COVID-19, as many people have gained access to education from anywhere in the world.

One of the key benefits is its flexibility, allowing students to attend classes from home and manage their schedules effectively. This accessibility also broadens global educational opportunities, enabling students worldwide to access top-tier institutions and quality education. Additionally, it offers convenience for those with busy schedules or health issues, making learning more inclusive.

Despite these hurdles, the potential of distance learning is immense, especially when combined with other methods like hybrid learning to enhance engagement.

As technology evolves, so will distance learning, ensuring its continued significance in educational development.
