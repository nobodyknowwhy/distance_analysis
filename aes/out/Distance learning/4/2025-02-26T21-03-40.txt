Distance learning has become an increasingly popular method of education in recent years. It refers to the process of studying remotely through technology like computers and the internet. One of its main advantages is that students can attend classes from anywhere in the world, which offers great flexibility. Another benefit is the wide range of subjects available online. Students can choose courses that match their interests or career goals without being constrained by physical location. Additionally, online learning often provides convenience for those who might otherwise struggle to attend traditional classes due to work or family commitments.
