Distance learning is an online method of education where students study remotely instead of going to school in person. It uses various platforms like websites or apps for virtual classrooms. This approach can be done synchronously, with real-time interaction, or asynchronously, allowing students to review materials at their convenience.

One major advantage of distance learning is its flexibility. Students can attend classes from home and schedule their study times as they wish. For example, online schools offer various courses that anyone can take without attending a physical institution. This method is also accessible to people who cannot attend traditional schools due to health issues or other personal reasons.
