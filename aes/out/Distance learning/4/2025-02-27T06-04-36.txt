**Essay on Distance Learning**

Distance learning has become an important way of studying these days. It's learning online from home. Because of the COVID-19 pandemic, many schools and colleges moved to teaching online.

One big advantage is flexibility. Students can study anywhere they want at any time. This helps those who cannot go to school due to health or other problems. Also, it makes learning more convenient because students don't have to worry about coming to a classroom.

However, distance learning also has some challenges. There might be technical issues like bad internet or old devices. Some students may get distracted by pets or siblings at home. Managing time is also hard without a routine.
