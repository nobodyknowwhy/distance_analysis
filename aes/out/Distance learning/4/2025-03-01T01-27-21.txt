**Distance Learning: A New Way of Learning**

Distance learning is an innovative method of education where students learn without physically being present in a classroom. Instead, they engage with courses online, often through video lectures and completing assignments from their homes. This shift has become especially prevalent during the COVID-19 pandemic when many schools had to switch to online learning.

One significant advantage of distance learning is its flexibility. Additionally, it offers diversity in subjects, enabling students to access courses that might not be available in their local area, such as specialized language courses or unique fields of study.
