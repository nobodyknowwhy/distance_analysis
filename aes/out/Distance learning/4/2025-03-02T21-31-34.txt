Distance learning is an educational method conducted online, allowing students to study from home or another location without attending traditional classrooms. With the rise of digital technology and the impact of events like the pandemic, its popularity has surged, offering numerous benefits and challenges.

One significant advantage of distance learning is flexibility. Students can attend classes at their convenience, fitting studies around work or family responsibilities. This adaptability often enhances productivity by enabling a better balance between personal life and academic pursuits.

Another benefit is accessibility. Distance learning bridges geographical gaps, making education available to remote areas that lack traditional schools. Additionally, diverse teaching methods are employed, such as e-learning platforms and virtual classrooms, which cater to varied learning styles.
