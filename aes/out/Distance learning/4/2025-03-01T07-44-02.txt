Distance learning is a method of education where students attend classes from different locations. Instead of being in the same classroom, they connect through technology like computers or televisions. This type of learning has become more popular because it gives people flexibility to study when and where they want.

One advantage of distance learning is that it allows people to balance their studies with their personal lives better. For example, students who have jobs or family responsibilities can attend classes online from home. They don’t have to wake up at the same time as their school class anymore. Some people even prefer this kind of learning because they find it easier to focus without distractions.

It can be hard to ask questions or make friends if you’re studying online from home.
