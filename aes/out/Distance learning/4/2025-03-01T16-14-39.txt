Today, it has become a prevalent method, offering flexibility, accessibility, and convenience to millions of students worldwide. This mode of education is particularly beneficial for those seeking to attend schools in distant locations or manage other commitments such as work or family responsibilities.

In conclusion, distance learning represents a transformative shift in the landscape of education, offering both opportunities and challenges.
