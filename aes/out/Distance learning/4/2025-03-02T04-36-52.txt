Distance learning has become more common in recent years. It is when people learn from their homes instead of going to a school or college. This type of learning allows students to study at any time they want, even if they are busy with work or family.

With the help of technology, distance learning can be done through computer or smartphone devices. Students can watch educational videos online, read books on the internet, and take tests from home. One benefit of distance learning is that it gives students more time to study. They don’t have to worry about traveling to school or missing classes due to illness. It’s also better than traditional school because they can learn at their own pace and ask questions whenever they want.
