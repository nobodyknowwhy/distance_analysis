Distance learning is a way people can study at home or from another place without going to school or university. It has become more popular in recent years, especially during times when schools are closed due to health problems.

One advantage of distance learning is that it gives students more freedom. They can learn anytime and anywhere they want. This is helpful for people who have jobs or families that take up their time.

Also, with the help of computers and the internet, people can access many courses online. They can study from home libraries or watch videos to learn new things. Some universities even offer classes through video calls so students can meet with teachers and other students online.

However, not everyone has good internet at home, which makes some people unable to study well. Also, some students find it hard to stay focused without a teacher telling them when to work.

Overall, distance learning is a useful way for many people to learn new skills or go back to school after being away from it for a while. But sometimes there are problems with bad internet or not staying focused.
