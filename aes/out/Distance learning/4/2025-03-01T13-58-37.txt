Distance learning is a way of studying without meeting face-to-face at school. It’s when you learn from home or online instead of going to class regularly.

In this type of learning, technology plays a big role because it brings education to people who can’t attend traditional schools. It gives students more freedom and convenience.

One benefit is that distance learning offers flexible timing for students. Some students might have busy schedules or health issues, so they can learn at their own pace. Also, you can access many different courses and resources that aren't available locally.

However, there are challenges too. Some students feel lonely because they don’t meet others in person. There might also be problems with internet connectivity making it hard to connect properly.

Despite these challenges, distance learning is becoming more common as technology advances. It’s important for teachers and students to stay connected to make sure everyone understands the material well. Overall, while it has its difficulties, distance learning can be very helpful for many people.
