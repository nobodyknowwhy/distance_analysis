**The Evolution and Impact of Distance Learning**

Distance learning has emerged as an innovative approach to education, offering students the flexibility to learn from home without strict schedules. This method gained significant traction during the COVID-19 pandemic, as many schools shifted to online platforms due to health concerns.

One of the primary advantages of distance learning is its convenience. Students can access courses at their own pace and from any location, providing a flexible learning environment. However, challenges such as technical issues—like unstable internet connections or reliance on devices—pose barriers for some learners.

Despite these challenges, distance learning has proven beneficial in enhancing educational outcomes.
