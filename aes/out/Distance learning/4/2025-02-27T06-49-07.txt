Distance learning has emerged as a transformative educational approach, revolutionizing how students access and engage with knowledge. Unlike traditional classroom settings, distance learning allows students to learn from anywhere in the world through various platforms and methods.

One of the most significant advantages of distance learning is its flexibility. This adaptability caters to diverse schedules, accommodating those who may be juggling work or family responsibilities. Additionally, the global reach of online courses breaks down geographical barriers, enabling students from remote areas or with disabilities to access high-quality education.
